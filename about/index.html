Our Mission

The AACC aims to enhance trade relations, investment opportunities, and economic growth between Asian and African collaborate, the chamber promotes mutual economic benefits and sustainable development.

By fostering a collaborative environment, the AACC provides a platform for businesses, governments, and organizations to connect and explore new markets. Through various programs and initiatives, the chamber facilitates trade missions, business forums, and networking events, which help members gain valuable insights and forge strategic partnerships.

Our
Impact

Our Impact

The AACC has successfully created numerous opportunities for businesses in both regions, strengthening international trade ties. The organizing committee for the CEO Conclave and Investors Dinner is composed of experienced professionals from the AACC, committed to delivering a high-impact event that aligns with their mission of fostering economic collaboration between Asia and Africa.

Over the years, the AACC has facilitated significant economic partnerships that have led to the creation of jobs, increased trade volumes, and enhanced cultural exchange between the two continents. Through initiatives such as trade fairs, business delegations, and investment forums, the AACC has enabled companies to expand their reach and access new markets.
