Exclusive CEO Conclave and Investors Dinner

Join us on November 15th at the prestigious Villa Rosa Kempinski for the CEO Conclave and Investors Dinner. This high-profile event aims to foster dialogue and collaboration among top executives, investors, and industry leaders.
Engage in networking, knowledge sharing, and explore investment opportunities in an exclusive setting.
The event brings together influential decision-makers from various sectors, facilitating strategic partnerships and driving business growth and innovation.

Mission and Purpose

The CEO Conclave and Investors Dinner, organized by the Asia Africa Chamber of Commerce (AACC), aims to foster a dynamic platform that bridges the gap between Asian and African markets, creating unparalleled opportunities for collaboration, investment, and sustainable growth.
Our mission is to bring together visionary leaders, influential investors, and innovative entrepreneurs from Malaysia, the UK, Australia, Nigeria, Singapore, and beyond, including local investors, to forge meaningful partnerships and drive economic transformation across these regions.

Organizers and Sponsors

The event is organized by the Asia Africa Chamber of Commerce (AACC), dedicated to fostering economic cooperation and business development between Asia and Africa.
The AACC enhances trade relations, investment opportunities, and economic growth through various events and initiatives.
