Exclusive CEO Conclave and Investors Dinner

Join us on August 16th at the prestigious Villa Rosa Kempinski for the CEO Conclave and Investors Dinner. This high-profile event aims to foster dialogue and collaboration among top executives, investors, and industry leaders.
Engage in networking, knowledge sharing, and explore investment opportunities in an exclusive setting.
The event brings together influential decision-makers from various sectors, facilitating strategic partnerships and driving business growth and innovation.

Mission and Purpose

The CEO Conclave and Investors Dinner aims to create a dynamic environment where top executives and investors can engage in meaningful dialogue, share visionary insights, and forge strategic partnerships.
Our mission is to accelerate business innovation, drive economic growth, and create lasting value by connecting industry leaders with investment opportunities.

Organizers and Sponsors

The event is organized by the Asia Africa Chamber of Commerce (AACC), dedicated to fostering economic cooperation and business development between Asia and Africa.
The AACC enhances trade relations, investment opportunities, and economic growth through various events and initiatives.

Platinum Package
  • Logo Placement: Prominent logo placement on event materials, including invitations, banners, and marketing collateral.
  • Keynote Introduction: Opportunity to introduce a keynote speaker or panel session.
  • VIP Seating: Reserved VIP seating for a specified number of company representatives.
  • Exclusive Networking: Host a private networking session during the event.
  • Exhibition Space: Premium exhibition booth placement in a high-traffic area.
  • Speaking Opportunity: Deliver a short address during the event.
  • Customized Branding: Brand visibility across various event platforms and media channels.
Gold Package
  • Logo Visibility: Logo placement on event materials and digital platforms.
